Common questions

Things people ask before they call.

How do you decide what to offer?

We look at recent comparable sales in your neighborhood, the cost of repairs needed, and our holding costs. We're transparent about the math and walk you through how we got to the number.

What if my house is in really bad shape?

That's exactly the kind of house we buy. Fire damage, water damage, structural issues, hoarding, code violations — none of it disqualifies you. We've seen worse.

How is this different from a real estate agent?

We're the buyer, not a middleman. There are no commissions, no listing time, no showings, no financing contingency. You get a guaranteed cash offer and pick the closing date.

What does it cost me at closing?

Nothing out of pocket. You pay no agent commissions, no closing costs, no inspection fees, and no repair costs. The number we offer is the number you walk away with at closing. We can offer this because we buy at a discount that covers the repairs, holding costs, and risk we take on after closing.

How does your offer compare to listing with an agent?

Honest answer: our offers are typically below what you might get listing on the open market over 60 to 90 days. We can pay less because we take on the repairs, the holding costs, the risk, and the months of waiting that a traditional sale requires. In exchange, you get certainty, speed, no repairs, and no fees deducted from the offer.

For homes that need work, sellers who need speed, or situations where the traditional process won't fit (probate, foreclosure, out-of-state, tired landlord), our offer often nets the same or more after you factor in everything you'd spend the other way: commissions, repairs, staging, holding costs, mortgage payments while it sits, and the months of your life it takes. We'll always tell you honestly whether we think a cash sale is the right path for your situation, even if the answer is no.

How fast can you actually close?

It depends on how quickly title work clears and how soon you're ready to move. Most cash sales take a few weeks once title is reviewed, but we work entirely on your timeline. Want to close as soon as possible? We'll move as fast as the paperwork allows. Need more time? Take all the time you need. You set the pace.

What if I have tenants in the property?

We buy occupied rentals. You don't need to evict anyone before selling. We step in as the new owner and handle tenant relationships with care — our goal is a smooth transition for everyone, not a disruption.
